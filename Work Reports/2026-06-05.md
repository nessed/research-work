## Key Findings

- PyMuPDF4LLM is the preferred Step 2 baseline because it is scripted, reproducible, page-counted, and less likely to rewrite source wording.
- Markdown output is usable for prose/commentary extraction.
- Tables, charts, images, and numeric values are weak zones and should not be trusted from Markdown without separate QA.
- Claude/Sonnet direct PDF-to-Markdown may be useful as a secondary aid for difficult pages, but not as the main reproducible pipeline.
